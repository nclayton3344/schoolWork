Title: 
	Credit Limit Calculator

Description: 
	Program to calculate a customer's account balance monthly.

Ingredients: 
	account_balance - customers balance at the beginning of the billing cycle
    credits - credits payed into customer's account for the billing cycle
    charges - charges for current billing cycle
    accountNum - customers account number
    credit_limit - customers credit Limit
    sentinel - to flag for repeat
    new_balance - remaining balance for current billing cycle

Instructions:
    Loop until user quits:
        Get account number, beginning balance, charges, credits, credit limit from user
        calculate new balance
        display balance and alert if new balance is greater than credit limit


Completeness:
	After calculating customer's balance, user must quit the program
